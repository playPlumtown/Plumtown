Three Kinds of Coin

In-game$PLUM — Plum

Earned endlessly by playing. Spend it in-game on furniture and upgrades. It is your score and your pocket money — fun, but it is not real money on its own.

VerifiedRedeemable Credits

Minted only by curated achievements the server recognizes — daily claim, quest completion, life milestones — and only once per account. The server is the sole authority, so a tampered client earns nothing. Daily & yearly caps apply.

On-chainReal SOL / USDC

When you withdraw, the backend debits your credits and sends real crypto straight to your Phantom wallet. You hold your own keys — the operator never custodies your funds.

Cashing Out — Step by Step

  1. 1
    Play & earn credits. Claim your daily reward and complete quests & milestones. Only server-verified achievements add Redeemable Credits.
  2. 2
    Sign in on the Community tab. Enter a display name to join the shared world — this links your progress to a server account.
  3. 3
    Connect Phantom. Open the Wallet tab → Redeemable Rewards → Connect Phantom and approve in your wallet to link & verify it.
  4. 4
    Withdraw. Hit Withdraw to wallet. The server deducts credits and sends real SOL/USDC. It appears in Recent Payouts with an explorer link.
Honest note: Real payouts are operator-funded and optional. They require the backend to be live with payouts enabled, are subject to minimums, daily caps and cooldowns, and may run in test mode (devnet) where coins have no real value. $PLUM alone can never be cashed out — only verified credits can. Play for the life sim first; the rewards are a bonus.
