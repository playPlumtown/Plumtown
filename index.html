FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Plumtown really free to play?

Yes. Account creation, gameplay, and earning are all free. You only pay network gas if you choose to withdraw $PLUM to an external wallet.

How do I earn $PLUM?

You earn by completing careers promotions, maxing skills, building relationships, finishing daily quests, and hitting lifetime aspiration milestones.

Is this pay-to-win?

No. There are no gameplay advantages for purchase. All progression is driven by your Sim's skills, mood, and the choices you make.

Do I need a crypto wallet to play?

No. You can play the entire game without one. A wallet is only needed to withdraw earned $PLUM off-platform.

Can I play on mobile?

The web app is fully responsive today. Native iOS and Android clients are scheduled for Q4.
